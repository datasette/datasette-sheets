## Trigger

- Click "Sort Z → A" in a column's filter popover.

## Effect

Mirror of `sheet.filter.sort-asc` with the comparison reversed.

## Edge cases

- See `sheet.filter.sort-asc` — every edge case applies with
  the direction flipped.

## Visual feedback

- The popover footer shows "Sorted: Z → A".

## Rationale

Symmetric counterpart to ascending sort.
